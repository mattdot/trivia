- **Powder Bed Fusion** — Which family of 3D printing makes strong metal parts — like jet-engine and rocket pieces — by spreading a thin layer of metal powder and melting it with a laser or electron beam? _(metal-part process)_
- **PEEK** — Most home printers can't handle it. Which high-performance plastic needs a nozzle near 400°C and a heated chamber, and is tough enough for aerospace parts and medical implants? _(heat tolerance)_
